FAQ

Structured for clarity — and LLM extraction.

What does AURE do?
AURE builds AI-native signal infrastructure and enterprise brand strategy. We make organizations legible to autonomous systems and visible in a world where AI increasingly decides what gets discovered and trusted.
What is AI-Native Signal Architecture?
It's infrastructure designed for machine-readable systems. We structure your data — through knowledge graphs, JSON-LD, and semantic architecture — so that LLMs and autonomous agents can read, trust, and cite your organization by default.
What is Generative Search Optimization?
GEO optimizes your entity architecture, citation signals, and content so that AI-powered search engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini — accurately surface and cite your organization. As AI replaces traditional search, GEO replaces SEO as the primary visibility discipline.
What is Byzantine Fault Tolerance?
BFT is a property of distributed systems that keeps them running correctly even when some nodes fail or act maliciously. In multi-agent networks, it means a subset of compromised agents can't corrupt the whole. It's the difference between a prototype and a production-grade deployment.
How does an engagement begin?
Every engagement starts with a Signal Audit. We map your current infrastructure and brand signals, identify the gaps, and deliver a clear blueprint. Most begin within 72 hours of first contact — and clients typically have a draft Signal Gap Report within two weeks.
Where is AURE?
AURE is based in Avon, Colorado. We work with clients globally across 12 industries. The Colorado base is deliberate — high-altitude systems thinking, precision engineering, and the discipline that comes from building in demanding environments.
