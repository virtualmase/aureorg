Framework

The five primitives
of ARM.

Every AURE deployment implements the Autonomous Resource Management framework — published by Mason Nguyen, formalized at autonomousresourcemanagement.com.

P1
Mandate Chain

Every autonomous decision is scoped within a cryptographically verifiable mandate issued by a human principal. Agents cannot act beyond their delegated authority.

P2
Signal Architecture

Resource state and allocation decisions are encoded in machine-readable structured data. The system is legible to both AI agents and human auditors at all times.

P3
Durable State

Every allocation decision is checkpointed. The system recovers to a known-good state from any failure mode without data loss or decision replay gaps.

P4
Graceful Escalation

When confidence falls below threshold, the system escalates to human review. Silence is never treated as approval. Every edge case has a defined path back to you.

P5
Immutable Audit Log

A complete, tamper-evident record of every allocation decision, mandate invocation, and escalation event. Required for compliance and post-hoc analysis.

What is AURE?
AURE (Autonomous Resource Management) deploys AI voice agents and builds signal infrastructure. We make organizations legible to autonomous AI systems and visible in the generative era — then deploy the agents that turn that visibility into revenue.
What is AI-Native Signal Architecture?
AI-Native Signal Architecture structures your data — through knowledge graphs, JSON-LD, and semantic architecture — so LLMs and autonomous agents can read, trust, and cite your organization by default. It's the foundation that makes every other AURE service work.
What is Generative Search Optimization (GEO)?
GEO optimizes entity architecture, citation signals, and content so AI-powered search engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini — accurately surface and cite your organization. As AI replaces traditional search, GEO replaces SEO as the primary visibility discipline.
What is Share of Model?
Share of Model is AURE's proprietary metric measuring how frequently your brand is cited by major LLMs in relevant query responses. It is the new market share for the agentic era — a number we track, report on, and optimize for every client.
What industries do you serve?
We work across 12 industries including fintech, healthcare, enterprise SaaS, legal, consulting, home services, logistics, and emerging technology. If you need to be machine-readable and you're losing revenue to missed calls or invisible brands, AURE can help.
How does an engagement begin?
Every engagement starts with a free Signal Audit. We map your current AI visibility, identify the calls and leads you're losing, and deliver a Signal Gap Report in 48 hours. No pitch deck — just data. Most full deployments begin within two weeks of the audit.
